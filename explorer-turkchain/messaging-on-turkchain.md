# Messaging on TurkChain

### Messaging Made Easy

**TurkChain Explorer Chat** is a wallet-to-wallet messaging feature that allows verified blockchain addresses to communicate directly with each other for outreach, coordination, and social interaction.

Messages are sent **off-chain**, securely, and without gas fees.

***

### What Is TurkChain Explorer Chat?

TurkChain Explorer Chat is designed to introduce **Web3-based messaging**, where users sign in with their wallet and communicate directly from address to address.

Instead of usernames or emails, your **wallet address is your identity**.

***

### Key Features

#### Instant Chat

Messages are delivered instantly between wallet addresses once both users are signed in.

***

#### End-to-End Encryption

All chat messages between signed-in addresses are **end-to-end encrypted by default**, ensuring privacy and message integrity.

***

#### Multi-Device Login

Access your conversations across multiple devices while using the same wallet address.

***

#### Block Addresses

Receiving unwanted messages?

You can block specific wallet addresses to prevent them from sending you messages.

***

#### Explorer Notifications

Get notified directly on **TurkChain Explorer** when your wallet address receives a new message.

***

#### Multichain Conversations

Start conversations with wallet addresses across supported **EVM-compatible chains**.

This allows communication beyond a single network while maintaining wallet-based identity.

***

#### Mobile Application

**New**

Enjoy a more immersive messaging experience on your mobile device by installing the TurkChain Explorer Chat mobile application.

***

### Frequently Asked Questions

#### What is TurkChain Explorer Chat?

TurkChain Explorer Chat is a wallet-to-wallet messaging system that enables blockchain users to communicate directly by signing in with their Web3 wallet.

***

#### Is there a cost to send messages?

No.\
Messages are sent **for free** because all communication happens **off-chain**.

No TURK gas fees are required.

***

#### Will I always receive a reply?

Not necessarily.

A reply depends on:

* Whether the recipient uses TurkChain Explorer Chat
* Whether they choose to respond

There is no guarantee of a response.

***

#### Is TurkChain Explorer Chat end-to-end encrypted?

Yes.

All messages are encrypted once **both addresses have signed in** to the chat system.

⚠️ **Exceptions** may apply to:

* Addresses using API-based automation
* Multisig or contract-controlled wallets

***

### Privacy & Responsibility

* Messages are not stored on the blockchain
* TurkChain Explorer does not read or modify message contents
* Users are responsible for how they communicate and who they trust

Always verify wallet addresses before sharing sensitive information.

***

### Final Note

Wallet-to-wallet messaging introduces a new layer of communication in the TurkChain ecosystem.

While powerful, always remain cautious:

* Do not share private keys
* Do not trust unsolicited offers
* Block and report suspicious addresses

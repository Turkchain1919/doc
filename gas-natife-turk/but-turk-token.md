# How to Buy TURK Token

The TURK Token sale is conducted through the official Ethereum-based sale interface.\
ETH and USDT purchases are recorded by a smart contract, and TURK tokens will later be claimed on **TurkChain**.

***

### 1️⃣ Connect Your Wallet

To participate, connect a compatible Web3 wallet:

* MetaMask or any EVM-compatible wallet
* The connected wallet address will be used for recording your purchase

> ⚠️ TURK tokens can be claimed **only by the same wallet** used during purchase.

***

### 2️⃣ Choose Payment Method

You can purchase TURK Token using:

* **ETH**
* **USDT (ERC-20)**

Enter the desired amount in USD:

* **Minimum:** 10 USD
* **Maximum:** 50,000 USD

The system automatically calculates the required ETH or USDT amount.

***

### 3️⃣ Complete the Purchase

* Click **Buy TURK (ETH)** or **Buy TURK (USDT)**
* Confirm the transaction in your wallet
* The transaction is completed on the Ethereum network

Each purchase is securely recorded by the sale contract.

***

### 4️⃣ TURK Token Distribution (Claim)

* TURK tokens will be distributed on **July 1, 2026**
* Distribution will be done via a **claim mechanism**
* Tokens are claimable **only by the purchasing wallet**

***

### ⚠️ Important Warning

❌ **Never send ETH or USDT directly to the contract address.**\
❌ Funds sent directly will be permanently lost.
